My Journey

I'm Felix Mandyme, a passionate software engineer with a unique journey into the tech world. When I matriculated, I wasn't sure which career path to take, but I knew I loved solving problems and learning new things.

I decided to explore my options at university, which led me to pursue a Bachelor of Agriculture at the University of Mpumalanga. During my second year, the COVID-19 pandemic changed everything. Witnessing the impact of technology in keeping the world connected, informed, and functioning sparked an "aha!" moment for me—I realized that my true passion lay in technology.

Determined to follow this new path, I completed my agriculture degree and immediately embarked on my journey into the world of computing.

Now, in 2025, I'm in my final year of a Bachelor of Computing, specializing in Software Engineering. My background gives me a unique perspective on problem-solving, adaptability, and innovation. I thrive on building efficient, scalable solutions and continuously expanding my knowledge in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
